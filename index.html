What We Can Help With

  • Managing, restoring, or cancelling a Premium subscription.
  • Questions about period, symptom, mood, weight, and intimacy logging.
  • Privacy requests, data export, data deletion, and account questions.
  • Bug reports, accessibility issues, and general product feedback.

Subscriptions

PeriodAI Premium subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. You can restore a purchase inside the app from the Premium screen, or manage and cancel subscriptions in Apple ID Account Settings on your device.

Privacy And Data

PeriodAI is designed for sensitive health journaling. Your in-app Privacy & Security tools let you export local data, erase local data, stop partner sharing, and manage consent.

Medical Disclaimer

PeriodAI is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease. Predictions and insights are educational estimates only. Contact a qualified healthcare professional for medical questions, persistent symptoms, or urgent health concerns.
