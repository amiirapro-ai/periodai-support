Delete your PeriodAI account

Last updated: July 19, 2026

You can request deletion of your PeriodAI account and associated data from the app or by email.

Delete from the app

  1. Sign in to PeriodAI.
  2. Open Settings, then Privacy & Security.
  3. Select Request account deletion and confirm.

Delete by email

Email hello@periodai.app from the address associated with your PeriodAI account. Use the subject “PeriodAI account deletion”. We may ask you to verify account ownership before processing the request.

Data deleted

Deletion covers the PeriodAI account profile and associated cycle, period, symptom, wellness, pregnancy, notes, assistant, preferences, partner-sharing, community, and subscription-status records under our control.

Retention

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days. Limited transaction, security, fraud-prevention, backup, or legal records may be retained only for as long as required by applicable law or legitimate operational obligations. Store subscription records managed by Google Play or Apple remain subject to the applicable store’s retention rules.
