Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

PeriodAI, published by Abbas Amira in the United Arab Emirates, helps users privately track cycles, periods, symptoms, wellness, pregnancy information, and optional partner-sharing preferences. PeriodAI is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or prevent disease.

Information we collect

We may process account information such as your email address and authentication identifier; health and wellness information you choose to enter; subscription and entitlement status; notes, assistant messages, sharing settings, preferences, and limited device or diagnostic information needed to operate and secure the service.

How we use information

We use this information to provide cycle tracking, symptom logging, personalized features, cross-device synchronization, premium access, privacy controls, account support, reliability, and security.

Service providers and sharing

PeriodAI does not sell personal data and does not use health information for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking. Authentication and storage are provided by Supabase. Subscription processing is provided by Google Play, the Apple App Store, and RevenueCat. We disclose information only as needed to provide these services, comply with law, protect users, or with your direction.

Security and retention

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. Access controls are designed to restrict records to the appropriate user. Account and health records are retained while your account is active and may be deleted when you request account deletion, subject to legal and operational retention requirements.

Your choices

PeriodAI provides controls to update consent, stop partner sharing, prepare a data export, log out, and request account deletion. You may also contact us to request privacy assistance.

Children

PeriodAI is not intended for children under 13, or under the higher minimum age required in their country. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below the applicable age.

International processing

Service providers may process information in countries other than your own. We use reasonable contractual and technical safeguards where required.

Changes

We may update this policy to reflect product, legal, or operational changes. The latest version and effective date will remain available on this page.
